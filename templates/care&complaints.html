RECOMMENDATIONS FOR USE

We are proud to know that our pieces are 100% handmade. Given their handmade nature, we suggest:

  • Hand wash.Although they are dishwasher safe, the pieces tend to deteriorate faster with this type of washing.  

  • Limit the use of microwaves. Although they are suitable for microwaving, the shine of the piece may deteriorate over time.

 

SHIPPING AND CLAIMS

  • If you received any broken or seriously damaged piece, we accept claims up to 5 days after the delivery of the order. Please write to us at barro.ceramic@gmail.com and to our WhatsApp so that we can resolve the replacement as soon as possible. In case we do not have stock of the purchased product, BARRO will propose  an alternative to fulfill the order on time.

  • Attention CABA and GBA orders - We have a logistics service that takes the products packed, door to door in a vehicle. This allows us to ensure that the parts arrive safely and without transport damage. For this reason, we will not accept quality claims on orders that have been received by intermediaries (porters, security, foreman, etc.), since we cannot ensure the condition of the products before they are delivered to our final customer. We suggest you receive the order yourself and open it at the time to ensure that all your pieces are in perfect condition.
