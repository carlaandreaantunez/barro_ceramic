About me, Sole.

I am Salvador and Vicente's mother, Kinesiologist and Yoga teacher, among other things. I teach ceramics courses: online and in person. This project started 3 years ago in Oscar's ceramics workshop, my teacher to whom I thank for everything I learned. I design my pieces looking for them to be unique, leaving all my love and dedication in these pieces.
